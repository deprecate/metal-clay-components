Images and Thumbnails

Responsive Image

Use .img-responsive to make an image shrink with its container.

<img alt="..." class="img-responsive" src="...">

Image Shapes

Use .img-rounded, .img-circle, or .img-thumbnail on <img> to quickly style the borders.

<img alt="..." class="img-rounded" src="...">
<img alt="..." class="img-circle" src="...">
<img alt="..." class="img-thumbnail" src="...">
