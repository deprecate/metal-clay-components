Extending Nameplates

Nameplate-label

Use .nameplate-label to reassure your users that they are sending to or selecting the right content/user.

JB
Joe Bloggs
SP
Space Program 
<div class="nameplate-label pull-left">
    <div class="nameplate-icon">
        <div class="user-icon user-icon-primary user-icon-xs">JB</div>
    </div>

    <div class="nameplate-content">Joe Bloggs <a class="icon-remove" href="#1"></a></div>
</div>
