Extending Cards

Use the cards component as a foundation to quickly mimic common patterns used throughout the web.

Space Program Category

Subscribe

Usage

Cards are a great way of summarizing a lot of related content, such as products, bookmarks, and favorites.

The card header and footer classes, were created to make it easier to style headers and footers across several cards, only one .card-header and one .card-footer should appear per card.

Use .card-section to group blocks of content and place between header and footer.

<div class="card">
    <div class="card-header">
        <div class="nameplate">...</div>
    </div>

    <div class="card-section">...</div>

    <hr class="divider" />

    <div class="card-footer">...</div>
</div>

Mystery Meal

Make content span full width

Don't wrap content in .card-header, .card-footer, or .card-section to make images and content span the full width of the card.

<div class="card">
    <div class="figure">
        <img class="img-responsive" src="thumbnail.jpg">
        <div class="figcaption-bottom figcaption-lg monospace">10 Most Delicious Space Foods</div>
    </div>
    <div class="card-section">
        <p class="text-default">Number 1</p>
        <h4>Mystery Meal</h4>
        <p>Because you like to live life on the edge of space.</p>
    </div>
</div>
Favorite Space Foods

Create equally spaced buttons with card-btn-row

Use .card-row and .card-btn-row to create equally spaced buttons inside a card.

<div class="card card-rounded">
    <div class="figure" style="margin-bottom:0;">
        <img class="img-responsive" src="thumbnail.jpg">
        <div class="figcaption-top figcaption-lg monospace">
            Favorite Space Foods
        </div>
    </div>

    <div class="card-row card-btn-row">
        <div class="card-col-4">
            <a class="btn btn-success" href="#1">
                <span class="icon-heart"></span>
            </a>
        </div>
        <div class="card-col-4">
            <a class="btn btn-success" href="#1">
                <span class="icon-bookmark"></span>
            </a>
        </div>
        <div class="card-col-4">
            <a class="btn btn-success" href="#1">
                <span class="icon-link"></span>
            </a>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

Travel to Space by Balloon

Space Program LLC is offering balloon rides to space!

Use img-absolute to make an image fill card-col

Make an image fill the width and height of a card column (image width and height must be larger than the column) by using .img-absolute and manually setting its top, right, bottom, or left position.

<div class="card-horizontal">
    <div class="card-row">
        <div class="card-col-5">
            <img class="img-responsive" src="thumbnail.jpg">
        </div>
        <div class="card-col-7 card-col-gutters">
            <h4>Travel to Space by Balloon</h4>
            <p>Space Program LLC is offering balloon rides to space!</p>
        </div>
        <div class="link-group">
            <hr class="divider" />
            <button type="button" class="btn btn-primary btn-xs">Sign Up</button>
            <button type="button" class="btn btn-link btn-xs">No Thanks</button>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
