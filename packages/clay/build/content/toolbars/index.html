Toolbars

Toolbar

Quickly prototype a toolbar for your portlet, control panel, or site with this component. Toolbar shouldn't be used to create a toolbar for your inline/text editing application; use btn-toolbar for that.

Each .toolbar-group should be able to stand on its own in mobile. If a .toolbar-group is too wide and looks squished on mobile, divide it into two toolbar-groups. Use .toolbar-group-right to align a toolbar-group to the right.

Content can be vertically aligned by adjusting the height of .toolbar-group, .toolbar-group-right, and form.toolbar-group-expand.

Vertically align text inside .toolbar-group-expand by matching the css property line-height to its sibling .toolbar-group.

Fill Remaining Space

Use .toolbar-group-expand with .toolbar-group and .toolbar-group-right to fill the remaining space with some content in your toolbar. .toolbar-group-expand must be the last toolbar-group declared.
