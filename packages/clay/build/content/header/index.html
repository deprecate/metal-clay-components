Header Toolbar

Header Toolbar

Use .header-toolbar when you need some actions with a one line heading that fills the remaining space.

.header-toolbar-left and .header-toolbar-right will only be as wide as its content.

.header-toolbar-center will fill the remaining width.

Adjust the size of the actions by adding padding to the anchor tag and matching the line-height in .header-toolbar-center to the height of the anchor.
