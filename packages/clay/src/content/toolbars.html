Toolbar

Quickly prototype a toolbar for your portlet, control panel, or site with this component. Toolbar shouldn't be used to create a toolbar for your text editing application; use btn-toolbar for that.

Each .toolbar-group should be able to stand on its own in mobile. If a .toolbar-group is too wide and looks squished on mobile, divide it into two toolbar-groups. Use .toolbar-group-right to align a toolbar-group to the right.
