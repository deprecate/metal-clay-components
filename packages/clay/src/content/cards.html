Horizontal Card

Display card content horizontally. .card-horizontal uses a simplified version of Bootstrap's grid system to help align content. It is based on the 12 column grid (default), but columns can be customized to any size.

Card columns do not break at smaller screen widths. They were designed to help align small chunks of content and are not meant to be used for page layouts.
