Header Toolbar

Header Toolbar is based on Toolbars, and was created to quickly prototype headings for portlets, mobile pages, and page sections. It comes in 6 different colorways, default, primary, success, info, warning, and danger. Colors can be customized by changing the global color variables in variables.scss or extending .header-toolbar with custom colors.
